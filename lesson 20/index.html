Konosuba is a manga adaptation with illustrations by Masahito Watari began publication in Monthly Dragon Age by Fujimi Shobo in September 2014. The audio drama was released in March 2015, and the anime adaptation aired from January to March 2016. Spin-off light novel Kono Subarashii Sekai ni Bakuen o! began publishing in July 2014. In addition, in December 2016, the official crossover was released with “Re: Zero. Life from scratch in an alternate world ", written jointly by the authors of both series. The second season of the anime series aired from January to March 2017.
